About Us

Welcome to Kabul News, your number one source for news in Kabul. At Kabul News, we are dedicated to providing you with the most accurate and up-to-date information about the happenings in and around Kabul. Our team is made up of experienced journalists and dedicated support staff who work tirelessly to bring you the stories that matter.

Founded in 2022, Kabul News has become a trusted name in local journalism, known for our in-depth analysis and comprehensive coverage of local events. Whether it's political developments, economic issues, or cultural stories, we cover it all with integrity and a commitment to journalistic excellence.
